Experience & Skills

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Nyiko Brilliant Hlungwani, an 18-year-old enthusiast deeply passionate about coding, 3D artistry, and music. As someone who excels in mathematics and relishes problem-solving challenges, I've found a fulfilling outlet in the realms of technology and creativity.

My journey in coding has been one of constant growth and discovery. From mastering languages like Python and JavaScript to delving into algorithmic problem-solving, I've honed my skills to craft efficient and elegant solutions. Whether it's developing applications, designing responsive websites, or diving into data analysis, I approach each project with enthusiasm and a keen eye for detail.

In the realm of 3D artistry, I've explored the fascinating intersection of technology and visual storytelling. Using tools like Blender and Maya, I've brought imaginary worlds to life, creating stunning visuals that captivate and inspire. From modeling and texturing to animation and rendering, I strive for realism and creativity in every project.

Beyond technology, music is my soulful escape. As a musician, I've composed melodies that resonate with emotions, blending genres to create unique soundscapes. Whether it's playing the piano, experimenting with electronic beats, or composing orchestral arrangements, music is where I find solace and creativity.

My portfolio is a testament to my skills, experiences, and relentless drive for improvement. I believe in continuous learning, drawing inspiration from diverse sources and collaborating with fellow enthusiasts. Together, let's create something extraordinary and make a lasting impact.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to connecting with you!
